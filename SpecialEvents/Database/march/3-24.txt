World Tuberculosis Day, observed on March 24 each year, is designed to build pub
lic awareness about the global epidemic of tuberculosis (TB) and efforts to eli
minate the disease. In 2012, 8.6 million people fell ill with TB, and 1.3 milli
on died from the disease, mostly in the Third World.World TB Day is one of eight
 official global public health campaigns marked by the World Health Organization
 (WHO), along with World Health Day, World Blood Donor Day, World Immunization W
eek, World Malaria Day, World No Tobacco Day, World Hepatitis Day and World AIDS
 Day.[2]
