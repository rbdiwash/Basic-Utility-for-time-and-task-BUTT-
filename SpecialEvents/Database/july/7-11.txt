World Population day is an annual event, observed on July 11 every year, which 
seeks to raise awareness of global population issues. The event was established
 by the Governing Council of the United Nations Development Programme in 1989. 
It was inspired by the public interest in Five Billion Day on July 11, 1987, the
 approximate date on which the world's population reached five billion people. W
orld Population Day aims to increase people's awareness on various population is
sues such as the importance of family planning, gender equality, poverty, matern
al health and human rights.
